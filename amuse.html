Patatap

This website transforms your keyboard into an amusing instrument with fun colors, lights, and sound. Visit the link provided and tap any of the letters on your keyboard to keep yourself occupied for AT LEAST 3 minutes.
Patatap.com

Clickhole

Are you tired of all the boring, or even worse, terrifying news articles that pop into your feed on a daily basis? Clickhole can provide you slideshows of the most random images you could possibly imagine, quizzes to tell you if you're capable to smuggle a king-size kit-kat into a movie theatre, and more! The articles are nearly endless and enough to keep you entertained for hours.
Clickhole.com

Zoom Quilt

If you need a good 5 minutes to shut your brain off, this is the site for you. This website zooms in on a picture neverendingly and there is always something more to see. The artwork combined with the endless hole of zooming will leave you hypnotized and substantially "zoned".
Zoomquilt.org
